About RNEx

Built on 4 Years of
Community Trust

RNEx Consultancy LLP was founded to bridge a gap that no digital platform could fill — trusted, face-to-face access to tightly-knit residential communities. We are not a marketing agency. We are your community partners.

Based at Raj Nagar Extension, Ghaziabad — one of NCR's most densely populated residential corridors — we have spent years building genuine relationships with residents, apartment associations, RWA officials, and local administration across 90+ complexes.

Our community network spans 100+ WhatsApp groups covering topics from business and health to carpooling, book exchange, blood donation, and more. When your business enters this network, it enters with a warm introduction — not a cold advertisement.

Local Map & Directory

Raj Nagar Extension's First Apartment-Level Directory

Leveraging Mr Deepanshu Mittal's 15+ years of professional cartography experience, RNEx is producing a printed Local Map & Apartment Directory distributed free to all residents across Raj Nagar Extension.

The directory features an apartment-wise locality map, local services guide, business classifieds, and sponsored sections — making it the most useful document a resident will keep at home all year.

For advertisers, it's unmatched visibility: not an ad that disappears in 5 seconds, but a physical page in every home across 90+ apartment complexes.
