About RNEx

Built on 4 Years of
Community Trust

RNEx Consultancy LLP was founded to bridge a gap that no digital platform could fill — trusted, face-to-face access to tightly-knit residential communities. We are not a marketing agency. We are your community partners.

Based at Raj Nagar Extension, Ghaziabad — one of NCR's most densely populated residential corridors — we have spent years building genuine relationships with residents, apartment associations, RWA officials, and local administration across 90+ complexes.

Our community network spans 100+ groups covering topics from business and health to carpooling, book exchange, blood donation, and more. When your business enters this network, it enters with a warm introduction — not a cold advertisement.
